100% Local

All your feeds, folders, and articles are stored in a high-performance local database. No cloud account required.

Powerful Feed Management

Organize feeds with folders, drag-and-drop reordering, and simple add, edit, and delete functionality.

Smart & Automatic

Automatic favicon fetching, background syncing, and data cleanup routines keep your library pristine.

Elegant UI & UX

Enjoy a classic three-pane layout, switchable splits, and light/dark modes with custom accent colors.
